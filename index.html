What is Manila 2104?

It is a Metroidvania game set in Dystopian Manila in the year 2104. Inspired by George Orwell’s 1984, players assume the role of a disgraced ex-military officer navigating a surveillance-heavy city to uncover buried truths about the authoritarian regime that betrayed him. With no melee combat, the protagonist relies on precise gunplay, stealth, and hacking mechanics to evade detection and survive. The game’s black-and-white aesthetic reflects the oppressive tone of the world, while pixelated rotoscope cutscenes drive the narrative forward, emphasizing emotional, cinematic moments.
